About Diya

Over the past few years, I've sat with people navigating the hardest career questions.

What should I do next? Am I heading in the right direction? How do I position myself for better opportunities?

Through 450+ one-on-one career guidance conversations and interactions with more than 4,000 individuals across workshops, training programs, classes, and career events spanning 7+ states across India — I've seen one thing repeatedly:

People often aren't lacking talent, potential, or ambition.
They're lacking clarity.

I've worked with students from government colleges in smaller cities, learners from India's most competitive institutions, fresh graduates entering the workforce, and professionals exploring new directions.

Different backgrounds. Different goals. But often the same feeling — being stuck between where they are and where they want to be.

Unstuck was built to bridge that gap. Not with generic advice. Not by telling you what to do. But by helping you understand your strengths, evaluate your options, position yourself effectively, and move forward with intention.
